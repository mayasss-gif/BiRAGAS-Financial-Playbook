# BiRAGAS · Financial Playbook

A coaching tool for defending the BiRAGAS 5-year financial model in an investor room. Single-page React app with audio narration on every section, modeled on [`BiRAGAS-Investor-Pitch-Guide-v2`](https://mayasss-gif.github.io/BiRAGAS-Investor-Pitch-Guide-v2/), distilled from the content of [`BiRAGAS-Financial-Projections`](https://mayasss-gif.github.io/BiRAGAS-Financial-Projections/).

**Live:** https://mayasss-gif.github.io/BiRAGAS-Financial-Playbook/

## What's inside

### Pitch Arc (10 sections)

1. **The Frame** — Bottom-up, customer-driven, testable. Not top-down market share.
2. **The Markets** — 27 markets, $1T+ TAM, three categories, structural diversification.
3. **The Streams** — Seven concurrent revenue motions across three diversification axes.
4. **The Ramp** — Customer cohort growth (47 → 562 in 5 years).
5. **The Pricing** — ASPs benchmarked to Recursion, Schrödinger, AbCellera 10-K disclosures.
6. **The Inflection** — 2028: $43M EBITDA, 25.6% margin, the crossover year.
7. **The Leverage** — Operating-leverage curve (OpEx ratio 235% → 56%).
8. **The Scenarios** — Bull / Base / Bear — all EBITDA-positive by 2028.
9. **The Risks** — Six risks, all with quantified mitigations.
10. **The Defense** — Why the model holds even in stress.

### Objections tab (8 financial Q&A)

Pre-cooked answers for the hardest financial questions: revenue aggression, EBITDA margin defense, customer-count realism, ASP benchmarks, concentration risk, sales-cycle assumptions, royalty modeling, FDA-timing sensitivity, upside-vs-downside surprise direction.

### Audio

`[🔊 ON / 🔇 OFF]` toggle in the header. Each section / objection plays an Ava Neural narration coaching the pitcher on tone, tactics, and specific numbers to quote.
